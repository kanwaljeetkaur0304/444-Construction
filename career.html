Full-Time

Drywall Taper

The Role:
We are looking for a detailed-oriented Drywall Taper to join our team. You are the final set of eyes on a project—the person who turns raw drywall into a perfect, paint-ready surface. If you take pride in a flawless finish, we want you.

What You'll Do:

  • Seal joints between drywall panels using tape and compound.
  • Apply successive coats of mud to achieve a smooth, seamless finish.
  • Sand joints and screw holes carefully to prepare walls for paint or wallpaper.
  • Inspect surfaces for any imperfections and fix them on the spot.

What You Need:

  • 2-5+ years of professional taping and finishing experience.
  • Mastery of hand tools (taping knives, trowels) and mechanical taping tools.
  • Expertise in achieving Level 4 and 5 finishes.
  • Strong attention to detail—errors will show, so precision is everything.
