Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

At 444 Construction, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website https://444constructionltd.ca/ and use our services.

Effective Date: January 01, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Project Details: Information regarding your construction or renovation needs, including site photos or blueprints you upload.
  • Communication: Any messages or inquiries sent through our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide estimates and fulfill construction services.
  • Communicate with you regarding project updates.
  • Improve our website and customer service.
  • Comply with legal obligations or building safety codes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third parties only when necessary, such as:

  • Subcontractors or suppliers involved in your specific project.
  • Permit offices or local government authorities as required by law.
  • Service providers who host our website or manage our email communications.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct or delete your data.
  • Opt-out of any future marketing communications.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as supplier websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites.
